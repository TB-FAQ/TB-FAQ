Everything about Safetrade

Q: What is Safetrade?

A: Safetrade is a simple system that allows you to trade items with other players witout the risk of getting scammed.



Q: Why should I use Safetrade?

A: Using Safetrade keeps you safe from scams, because both parties must agree to the trade before it is finished. It works by sending a request for the items/TC you wish to trade to a recipient, and letting them suggest what they would trade back for, allowing for both parties to then accept or reject the trade before it is made.



Q: How do I use Safetrade?

A:

  1. Go to the Torishop and select "Safetrade" under "Account" in the navigation bar on a left hand side.
  2. You will now see the safetrade page. Proceed with the instructions provided by the system, which inclue these steps:
    1. Add the username of the recipient
    2. Add a message to send to the recipient (not required)
    3. Select items or TC that you wish to trade
    4. Click "Start Safetrade"
  3. Wait for the reply from the person you're trading with. You will be notified by private message when recipient responds to your trade
