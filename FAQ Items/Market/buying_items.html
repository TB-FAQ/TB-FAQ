Q: Do items make you better in game?

A: No. Items provide only cosmetic enhancements.

Q: How can I get items?

A: All items cost Toricredits (TC) and can be bought in the Market from other players or from the Torishop. Some items are not available for TC in the ToriShop and can only be purchased with real currency. Most items require you to have a minimum amount of Qi before you can purchase or receive the item, and you need a minimum of 20 Qi to trade at all. The Qi requirement for an item can be found in the ToriShop. You may purchase an item with real currency even if you do not meet the Qi requirement.

Q: How do people earn all the TC for these items?

A: There are several ways to gain TC.
  • Winning in game on official servers nets you 10 Toricredits (or more, if you have boosters), per win.
  • Winning in game tournaments can net you anywhere from 50 to 10,000 (or more!) Toricredits!
  • Winning events can net you serious Toricredits depending on the event prizes. Check the Events board for details.
  • Trading items in the Market can be very profitable.
  • You can also make and sell artwork for signatures, avatars, and textures in the Market boards.

Q: What about flames?

A: For flames, you can go here to buy pre-made ones, as well as going ingame and going to the menu screen and clicking Setup > Utils > Flame Browser.

If you go ingame and hit escape, or are on the menu screen, you can go Setup > Utils > Flame Forger to make your own flames.

A flame can have various effects depending on the way the forger manipulates it, but it it always centered around a chosen joint.

When you have a flame, you can also apply an image to the particle with a flame particle texture. Flame particle textures make each particle represent the image you apply to it.
Note: without a flame, the flame particle texture does nothing!
