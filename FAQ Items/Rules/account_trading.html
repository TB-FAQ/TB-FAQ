Account Trading

Q: Am I allowed to buy/sell or otherwise trade my account in any way?

A: No. All trading of accounts is completely prohibited. If an account is traded, it will be banned, along with the buyer and seller.



Q: I was scammed while trading my acocunt.

A: You are not allowed to trade your account in any way. The account which was traded will be banned. Toribash Community Staff will not support account trading, and you will not be helped if you are scammed while trading your account.



Q: Is account sharing allowed?

A: No. Account sharing is considered to be indistinguishable from account trading, and is thus is also banned.
