Account Trading

Q: Can I legally buy/sell or otherwise trade my account in any way?

A: No. All trading of accounts is completely prohibited - if you are caught trading them you will be banned.



Q: HELP! Someone scammed my account in the selling process!

A: Nabi and Toribash don't support account trading, in-fact we are now going to ban you for admitting you broke the rules.



Q: Is account sharing allowed?

A: No; account sharing is illegal and is considered a bannable offense too.
