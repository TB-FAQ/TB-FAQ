Reporting Rulebreaking

Q: How do I report someone who is breaking the rules?

A:

  • If you have been scammed, please read the Scam Report guidelines, and then submit a scam report in the Scam Reports board
  • If the rulebreaking took place in a post or private message on the forums, please use the Report button on their post, just below their avatar.
  • If you believe that a staff member has broken the rules or has otherwise behaved inappropriately, please contact them directly about the situation first. If even after they have been given the opportunity to explain themselves you feel that they are still performing inappropriately as a staff member please post in the Staff Complaints board.
  • For any other rule violations or concerns, please post in the general Reports board.
For us to process a report, evidence must be provided with the report. For scams, this would include screenshots of the deals involved. For any complaints regarding ingame activity, this would include screenshots of the rule infracting activities that cover enough to fully explain the situation. When reporting posts or private messages, any information not immediately present in the post or private should be added to the report message.

Q: Someone is doing something that I think is against the rules but I'm not sure, can I report them?

A: Absolutely. We may discard your report if we find that their behavior isn't actually harmful, but you do not need to know specifically which rule they are breaking to report them If someone is making you uncomfortable ingame, or seems to be trying to incite drama unnecessarily, feel free to report them. The Toribash Community Staff may or may not decide to take action, but you will not be punished for filing a report unless the report was intentionally meant to be a waste of our time.

If you are reporting too much, we'll tell you. If you haven't heard from us that you're reporting too much, then we don't think you are.

Q: I don't see any posts in the (Scam) Reports board, is it actually used?

A: Yes.
The Reports and Scam Reports board are set so that only you and staff members can see your posts in that board. If you are not a staff member, you will not be able to see any other user's reports. Furthermore, other users, including the user you are reporting, will not be able to see yours. With the exception of Staff Complaints (where all parties involved will be publicly available), reports you make will be kept between you and the Toribash Community staff to the best of our ability to do so.

Q: How long will it take for my report to be looked at?

A: The Toribash Community staff are people too. We will do our best to handle reports in a timely fashion, but due to timezones and activities it may be up to 24 hours before a report can be looked at. In the case of immediate issues, such as issues in an ingame room, you may join the #support channel on IRC and ask for assistance there.
