Boosters and Subscriptions

Q: What are subscriptions?

A: In Toribash, you can purchase subscriptions for your account.

A subscription is a one-time or repeating payment which will add extra features to your account.

Some subscriptions will affect your forum profile, whereas others (called boosters) may give you extra TC/QI for winning games. You can see all the available one-time subscriptions here, and repeat subscriptions here!

Forum subscriptions:

  • Forum VIP ►
    • This subscription adds a ton of features to your forum experience, including:
    • Bigger 150x150 px and animated avatars
    • Have more room (up to 6 lines) and use images in your signature
    • Forum name prefix of your choice
    • Post background images
    • Global forum search functionality
    • Forum VIP exclusive board access
  • Wibbles Access ►
    • This subscription will give you access to the (otherwise hidden) wibbles board for 30 days. Wibbles is a completely off-topic board with very little moderation. This subscription is non-repeating and must be re-purchased at the end of its lifespan.
  • Wibbles Anonymity ►
    • Buying this causes all your posts in the Wibbles board to become anonymous - all your identifying details will be hidden. THIS WILL NOT GIVE YOU ACCESS TO WIBBLES BY ITSELF - SEE THE 'WIBBLES ACCESS' SUBSCRIPTION FOR THAT. This subscription is non-repeating and must be re-purchased at the end of its lifespan.

In-Game subscriptions:

  • QI Booster ►
    • Buying this subscription will make you earn twice as many Qi points for each fight during a month.
    • Please note this does not raise the daily Qi limit of 100 games. You will merely reach this limit twice as fast.
  • TC Boosters ►
    • Buying a TC booster will increase the amount of TC you earn per win for a month. The amount won depends on the specific booster you bought. TC boosters will only increase your earnings in any room until you reach the daily 100 boost (wins) limit. You can continue playing past the 100 Qi limit, but it won't count towards your Qi any more.


Q: My subscription didn't activate!

A: Email support@toribash.com or contact hampa via PM. When emailing support@toribash.com, please be patient.

If you don't get a response within three days, feel free to shoot an email again.



Q: How can I cancel a repeat subscription?

A: Follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your PayPal account and go to the "History" subtab of the "My Account" tab.
  2. Choose "Subscriptions" from the pull-down "Show" menu.
  3. Press the "Submit" button. Choose this subscription, and click on its "Status."
  4. You will be taken to a Transaction Details page from which you may cancel your subscription.
