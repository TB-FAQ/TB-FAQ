Email and Account Activation

Q: How do I register an account for Toribash?

A: Registration is easy, and when you register on the forums or in game, your account will work both on the forums and in game.

How to register with Toribash:

  1. Click 'Register' up in the top left on the black bar.
  2. Fill out all required fields.
    • Keep in mind your password must be at least 8 characters long; this is for your own safety. You can learn more about how to make a good password here: [LINK TO PASSWORD SECURITY FAQ ITEM].
    • Its highly recommended that you use a legitimate email address to register your account. This will help us secure your account and allow us to easily recover your password for you.
  3. That's it! Your new account is now registered and will work both on the forums and ingame.

Q: How do I login to the forums?

A: Assuming you have a registered account (see above if you don't have one), logging in is extremely easy:

How to login to the forums:

  1. At the top right of any forum page, find the fields requiring your username and password
  2. Fill these in, and click 'Login'
  3. Done!
