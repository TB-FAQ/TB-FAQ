Clan Wars

Q: What is a clan war?

A: A clan war is a in-game fight between two different clans that are recorded and effect your clan xp, and position on the clan list. They have a time limit of 1 week, and play on a most out of/first to point system.


Q: How do I start a clan war?

A: The first step to creating a war, is being in a clan; The next step is going to the wars page which can be located here, or on the side clan bar, or even under your clan's name, next you are ready to create a clan war, the interface may look scary at first, but it's easy to use once you understand it.

The first thing you will notice is the Guest Clan box, this is the name of the clan you want to war; Next is the Game Limit box, this is the amount of games total that the war will last; Finally we have the First To box, if this is set as 0, the war will win based on a most-out-of system, if it is used, then it will be a first-to system; Finally you can click the "Declare War" button to which the guest clan you have chosen earlier, will need to accept it; After the guest clan has accepted the war invite, you will need to click the Load Items button under the "War Setup" section, both clans will need to click a "Accept Items"/"Accept Prizes" button, and finally, the war will start in the current in-game room you are in


Q: What's next?

A: The next, but final step, is playing the war in game, sometime's it'll require you to play an extra match before the war will actually begin, spectating all players in the room and entering again will also work; Now, let the war begin!


Q: Where can i see my previous wars?

A: Under the "War Setup" section, you can see a Event History section, this is where you can see all your previous wars, On the right of the setup section you can also see your current wars, and most recent war.
