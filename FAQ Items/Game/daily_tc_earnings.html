Daily TC Earnings

Q: How much TC can I earn per day from playing the game?

A: You can earn TC from 100 games per day maximum, which makes 1000 TC total per day. Earnings from market, duels etc. are not restricted.

Q: Is my daily TC still restricted if I have a booster?

A: Yes.

Q: But I play more than 100 games per day!

A: Bear in mind that you only gain TC when you win.
