Toribash Staff

Q: Who are the Toribash community staff?

A: The community is managed by several types of staff, each with their own role and focus. Staff is generally divided into two catagories: lower staff (Squad members) and higher staff (SMods and above).

You can see a full list of staff here.

Name Forum Colour In-Game Symbol In-Game Permission Role
Administrator Red
#FF0000		 ~ Admin
Dark Red		 Same as Super Moderators (below), with a focus shift toward policy dicussion and projects, as well as providing seniority for the other groups; admins are almost always the lead of a lower staff group. Administrators can also manage other usergroups, modify the forum and have full edit permissions for user profiles (they're therefore the port of call for getting namechanges). This group has many more roles and permissions beside the ones listed here, and some admins will have permissions other don't.
Clan Management Black
#000000		 ^ Admin
Dark Red		 Oversee Clan Squad and perform the same duties with some additional roles. Clan Managers can edit Toriclans themselves, and are also more involved with activity checking than Clan Squad. Clan Management are the ones who vote on Organizations/Clans trying to become official. They're also the port of call to resolve clan war issues. They are also capable of infracting and banning users.
Super Moderator Blue
#0000FF		 ~ Admin
Dark Red		 Moderate the entire forum, partake in community-wide policy discussion, provide senior guidance for the lower groups, give out profile infractions, handle general reports, be on-call to resolve in-game issues.
