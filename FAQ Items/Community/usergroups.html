Usergroups

Q: What are usergroups?

A: Any account registered in Toribash belongs to one or more usergroups - a usergroup defines what permissions and customisation the account has. There are actually quite a number of usergroups, although not all of them are attainable or used.

Usergroups stack on top of eachother, and 'allow' permissions override

Q: What groups are currently in use? What do coloured names mean?

A: Currently active usergroups:

Standard Usergroups ►
Name Colour Description
Unregistered None Unregistered users can view only parts of the forum, and can't interact with it at all. They also can't play multiplayer (but they can play singleplayer).
Registered Default The base usergroup; everyone who has made an account for Toribash belongs to this group. Grants generic permissions such as the ability to use basic forum features and play in multiplayer.
Forum VIP Default An extension of the Registered group, VIP allows access to search functions, a bigger avatar, access to a new board and more. See here for more details.
Suspended (banned) Default Suspended accounts have nearly all their access permissions revoked. They cannot play in multiplayer or interact with the community in any way outside of an appeal thread (only viewable by themselves and staff) they can optionally make if they believe their suspension is in error.
Other Usergroups ►
Name Colour Description
(Site) Developer Default Site devs are users who add and maintain the features of the forum.toribash.com/* site. They don't have any special permissions on the live site beyond access to certain boards.
Event Artists Default Event Artists are users with an artistic talent, who work with Event Squad (or any staff group in need) to provide art assets to be used for events etc.
Event Modders Default Event Modders are users with the talent and dedication to put together amazing mods to be used in events by the Event Squad proper.
Legend Gold
#BB9600		 Torilegends are users who have contributed something great to the community. Amazing artists, tireless ex-staff, outstanding replay-makers; this group is to honor them. See more here.
Ninja ? Super admins. Who are they? Where are they? You'll know if they want you to know. This usergroup represents the most senior level of staff; they are the final word when it comes to policy decisions, community projects or anything else you'd care to think of (not everything will need a ninja's go-ahead though).
Old Schoolers Default Oldschoolers are users who've been around since before the distant time of 2008. This grants them access to a special board just for them, as well as a swanky + in-front of their names in-game.
